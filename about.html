About Gintaras

Gintaras is a free app for learning Lithuanian vocabulary through short, game-like lessons — flashcards with audio pronunciation, quizzes, listening practice, and a matching game. It's built for absolute beginners: people reconnecting with Lithuanian family roots, travelers, or anyone curious about the language.

The name "Gintaras" means amber in Lithuanian — a nod to the Baltic coast, one of the world's richest sources of amber.

What's inside

Ten themed units covering greetings, numbers, family, food, colors, time, body parts, and everyday verbs — around 80 core words to start with, with more being added regularly.
